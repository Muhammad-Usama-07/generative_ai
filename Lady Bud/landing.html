Your data stays yours

This is some of the most personal information you have. Bloom is being built with that as a first-class constraint, not an afterthought.

  • No diagnosis — ever. Educational content only, with clear pointers to a professional for anything symptom-specific.
  • No selling or sharing of health data with third parties.
  • Encrypted storage once the backend lands — nothing sensitive sits in plain text.
  • You can clear your data at any time.
